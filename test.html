THE PROJECT PROCESS:

STAGE 1: Understand the concept - when a project is conceptualised, we've got a plan

STAGE 2: Preperation and documentation - We manage the preparation of the concept design, initial costing and high level timelines

STAGE 3: Procurement - Managing the compiling of the construction contract and the handover of the site from the Client to the Contractor

STAGE 4: Expect the unexpected - On any project there are unknowns and unforeseen events. Our approach tackles these proactively and deals with issues as they arise

STAGE 5: Construction - M3 Africa monitors the progress of the Contractor against their proposed programme, applying rigorous pressure and oversight to maximise efficiency
